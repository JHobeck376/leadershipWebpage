Though this is a controvercial decision, I believe the good would be amazing, as it would require students to talk to those that they haven't before so that they can know if they need help and if they could write them a warm fuzzy.

What did you learn from your conversation about leadership?

I interviewed my coding teacher, Mr. Clawson, to get his perspective on leadership, as he is the leader for our CTSO program. I learned that some very important parts of being a leader is that they should work for the people and for the people alone. They should work on the forefront, showing their people how it should be done. He also said that a leader should be the first to arrive, but the last to eat and the last to leave. These are all incredible points, and all should be observed in leadership.
